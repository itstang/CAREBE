ULTIVA

*(remifentanil HCI)

IV Analgesia

Why should anesthesiologists choose ULTIVA?

Rapid On

Remi* gives you flexibility when you want predictable control of depth and duration of analgesia.

Rapid Response

Easily titratable dosing

Remi* provides rapid response to dose adjustments in 5-10 minutes for predictable control of analgesic coverage.

Rapid Off

Unlike other IV opioids, quick clearance and lack of drug accumulation result in rapid dissipation of respiratory depressant and analgesic ffects (within 5-10 minutes) upon discontinuation of ULTIVA at recommended doses.

Rapid Recovery

In diverse inpatient and outpatient populations, Remi* was associated with a somewhat faster emergence than fentanyl.

Faster emergence was accompanied by:

  • Earlier response to verbal command
  • Earlier discharge from the operating room for outpatients
  • Earlier eligibility for discharge home for outpatients

No Accumulation

Remi* is rapidly metabolized by nonspecific blood and tissue esterases1.

  • Unique organ dependent metabolism allows use in patients with renal or hepatic impairment1
  • Offset independant of infusion duration to have patients alert quickly even after long procedures1,2
  • No significant accumulation in adipose tissue1,3

Synergistic Effect

In clinical studies, Remi* reduced the dosage of other drugs used for loss of consciousness:

  • Propofol1
  • Thiopental1
  • Desflurane2
  • Etomidate2
  • Isoflurane3
  • Enflurane3

Hemodynamics

Remi* has a well established Hemodynamic Profile.

Effects on blood pressure and heart rate have been well established in clinical studies.
